# Retail Sales & Profit Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales performance using SQL and Power BI. The goal of this project is to identify key insights related to sales, profit, and loss-making products across different regions and product categories. The dashboard helps understand business performance and supports data-driven decision making.

## Dashboard Preview
![Dashboard](dashboard.png)

## Tools Used
- SQL – Data analysis and aggregation  
- Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation  

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales: 1.10M  
- Total Profit: 132.52K  
- Profit Margin: 12.05%  
- Loss Products Count: 529  

## Key Insights

1. Technology category generates the highest revenue and profit.  
Technology contributes the largest portion of both sales and profit compared to other product categories.

2. Furniture category has high sales but very low profit.  
Although furniture generates significant sales, its profit is very low, indicating potential issues such as high costs or heavy discounting.

3. West region records the highest sales.  
Among all regions, the West region contributes the highest revenue, while the South region shows the lowest sales performance.

4. Tables and Bookcases are major loss-making sub-categories.  
These sub-categories generate significant negative profit, suggesting pricing or cost management issues.

5. Sales and profit margins fluctuate across months.  
Monthly analysis shows variation in sales and margin trends, indicating possible seasonal demand patterns.

6. Several products generate negative profit.  
Multiple products contribute to losses, which could require price adjustments or inventory strategy improvements.

## SQL Analysis
SQL queries were used to perform data analysis and calculate key metrics before building the Power BI dashboard.

Key SQL analysis includes:
- Total Revenue calculation
- Total Profit calculation
- Profit Margin percentage
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Loss-Making Products identification
- Top 5 Customers by Sales
